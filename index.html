Bayes Rule Calculator

A simple Bayes rule calculator.

Put the known probabilities in the fields below, click the "Calculate Bayes Rule" button, and see the result of calculating Bayes rule.

P(H) = (prior)
P(D | H) = (sensitivity)
P(¬D | ¬H) = (specificity)
 
 
(1 - prior) = P(¬H)
(1 - sensitivity) = P(¬D | H)
(1 - specificity) = P(D | ¬H)
 
 
(prior × sensitivity) = P(H, D) = P(H) × P(D | H)
((1-prior) × (1 - specificity)) = P(¬H, D) = P(¬H) × P(D | ¬H)
 
(normalizer for D) = P(D)
 
 
(prior × (1-sensitivity)) = P(H, ¬D) = P(H) × P(¬D | H)
((1-prior) × specificity) = P(¬H, ¬D) = P(¬H) × P(¬D | ¬H)
 
(normalizer for ¬D) = P(¬D)
 
 
= P(H | D)
= P(¬H | D)
 
= P(H | ¬D)
= P(¬H | ¬D)
